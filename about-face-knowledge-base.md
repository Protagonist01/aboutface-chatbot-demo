## 14. APPLICATION TIPS & TRICKS

### Eyes

**Matte Fluid Eye Paint**:
1. Prep eyelids with Shadow Fix Eyeshadow Primer
2. Wait 20-30 seconds for primer to dry
3. Apply Matte Fluid Eye Paint with fingers or favorite tool
4. Work one area at a time (formula dries quickly)
5. For diffused look: Use fingertips or fluffy brush to blend edges
6. Add sparkle: Layer Fractal Glitter Dust or Gelly on top
7. As eyeliner: Gently tap (don't sweep) with stamping motion using angled brush

**Line Artist Eyeliner**:
1. Start at inner corner of eye
2. Place pencil as close to lash line as possible
3. Glide across lash line for precise line
4. For smoky look: Blend out before formula dries
5. To sharpen: Pull cap off back of pencil and twist clockwise

### Lips

**Matte Fix Lip Pencil**:
- **Subtle lipstick look**: Tilt pencil at angle and glide across lips after shaping
- **Lock in color**: Top with lip balm or Light Lock Lip Gloss in "Not All At Once"
- **Cupid's bow definition**: 
  1. Draw an "X" starting at highest point on one side
  2. Draw diagonal line along inner curve of lip
  3. Repeat on other side
  4. Apply lip color within the lines

### Face

**The Performer Foundation**:
1. Twist, wiggle, and gently pull applicator out
2. Oversized doe foot has just the right amount for medium coverage
3. Apply to areas desired
4. Blend with brush, sponge, or fingers

**The Illusionist Concealer**:
1. Use functional paddle applicator
2. Glide, sweep, press, and blend seamlessly
3. Delivers exact amount needed with every swipe
4. Can be layered for buildable coverage

**Light Lock Highlight Fluid**:
- Just a drop needed
- Mix with foundation or moisturizer for natural glow
- Layer above makeup for pop of radiance
- Shake well before use

## 22. TROUBLESHOOTING GUIDE

### Common Product Questions

**Q: My Matte Fluid Eye Paint dried too quickly before I could blend.**
A: Work one area at a time and use fingertips or a fluffy brush immediately after application to diffuse edges before it sets.

**Q: How do I sharpen my Line Artist eyeliner?**
A: Pull the cap off the back of the pencil to access the built-in sharpener. Twist the liner in a clockwise motion.

**Q: Can I sharpen my Matte Fix Lip Pencil?**
A: Yes, use any standard cosmetic sharpener.

**Q: My foundation shade oxidizes/changes color.**
A: The Performer Foundation is designed with true color affinity to minimize oxidation. Make sure you're using the correct undertone for your skin. Try the shade matching quiz.

**Q: How much highlighter should I use?**
A: Just a drop of Light Lock Highlight Fluid is needed. The formula is highly concentrated.

**Q: Can I mix different About-Face products together?**
A: Yes! The brand encourages experimentation. You can mix highlighter with foundation, layer glosses over matte lips, use eye products in creative ways, etc.

### Order Issues

**Q: My order hasn't shipped yet.**
A: Orders placed by 12pm EST process same day. Standard processing is 1-2 business days. High-volume periods may experience delays. Check your email for tracking updates.

**Q: I received the wrong product.**
A: Email help@aboutface.com immediately with your order number. They will send a replacement.

**Q: My product arrived damaged.**
A: Contact help@aboutface.com with your order number and photos of the damage. All items leave the warehouse in excellent condition.

**Q: My package says delivered but I don't have it.**
A: Wait 48 hours as the carrier may correct the delivery or a neighbor may bring it. After 48 hours, contact help@aboutface.com for a replacement.
